: Features of Shooter in this program             
- spring constant 719.43 N/m                
- shooting range  1 to 4 m
- angle range 15 to 80 dagree
: Notice
1. basket has 41 cm height and ball has 24 g mass
2. This simulation regardless of air resistance
3. Initial position when shoot (x, y) = (0, shooter's end point height)
4. Distance which can be set is the distance of initial point and the center of the basket
